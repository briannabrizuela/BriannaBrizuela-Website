My name is Brianna Brizuela and I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside. I was born, raised and currently live in Riverside, and I plan on completing my college education to give back to my community as a secondary education history teacher. History has always been very interesting to me and I believe it's the best teacher. I would like to encourage students to take a chance on history as a teacher. I am working towards this goal by actively taking 15 or more units per quarter. As a first generation college student, I would really like to graduate in four-years. I also plan on obtaining my teaching credentials and hopefully substituting with local school districts after graduation.
For experience, I have recently worked as a sales clerk at the Dollar Tree. There I gained skills in customer service, handling purchases and returns, and answering questions about products, policy, and services. I am also an active volunteer in my community. I volunteer with Fremont Elementary's PTA and with a local Girl Scout troop. At Fremont Elementary, I assist at school events by setting up and breaking down the event and also helping run the event. I have also volunteered as the school mascot on the first day of school. After serving my community as a Girl Scout for 12 years, I aged out and became a lifetime member. I now assist my former troop leader with her troop by helping at meetings and providing insight on my Girl Scout experiences. All of these experiences have provided me with strong leadership skills, organization and time management skills and a strong work ethic. I work very hard on any task given to me and I really put my all into it. I strive to do my absoulute best with everything to prove my capabilities. I believe all of these experiences have allowed me to demonstrate my committment to tasks and responsibilites, while also helping me develop these skills further.

Personally, I enjoy camping, baking and traveling. Additionally, I like to listen to music and watch classic movies.
